Dr. Anmol Rattan Sidhu served as Assistant Solicitor General of India, Additional Advocate General, Haryana, President of the Punjab and Haryana High Court Bar Association, Chairman of the Bar Council of Punjab and Haryana, and Deputy Advocate General, Government of Punjab. Dr. Sidhu served as the Dean, Faculty of Law, Punjab University, Chandigarh. He was awarded the PARMAN PATRA by the Punjab Government in the year 2004 for his contribution to society. This is the highest Award given to a Civilian of the State in recognition of his/her services.
