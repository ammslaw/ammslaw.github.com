Advocate Supreme Court of Pakistan
Member of the American Bar Association

Arif Chaudhry has been practicing law for the past 30 years. He has been enrolled as a Legal Consultant for the State of New York since 1991. He served as Legal Consultant, Ministry of Interior, Government of Pakistan and Legal Consultant, Pakistan Housing Authority. He also served as Senior Consultant to the Ministry of Law, Justice and Parliamentary Affairs, Deputy Attorney General for Pakistan, Legal Advisor for the Capital Development Authority, Lahore Development Authority, Punjab Cooperative Board for Liquidation, Punjab Tourism Development Corporation, Punjab Local Government Board, Local Government & Rural Development Department Punjab, and PASSCO. He also served as the Vice Chairman of the Punjab Bar Council. Mr. Chaudhry represented the Head of State of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan and Head of the Government of Pakistan (both while in office) as their Legal Counsel in a private capacity. He conducted a large number of cases on behalf of various government and autonomous departments and organizations.
